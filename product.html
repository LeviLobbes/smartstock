The Product

A camera that
watches your shelves.

It mounts to the ceiling, monitors your products, and tells your team when something needs attention. No special IT setup. Plug it in and it works.

How It Works

Five steps. That's it.

1

We install the cameras

Small cameras mount to the ceiling above your shelves and food areas. One camera covers up to 6 shelf bays. Connects with a single ethernet cable.

2

It learns your store

Within 3 days, SmartStock knows where every product goes. No one has to program it. It figures out your layout by watching.

3

It spots empty shelves

The camera checks every shelf every 30 seconds. When something's missing, it knows within a minute. No more walking the floor to find gaps.

4

Your team gets a phone alert

The right person gets a notification on their phone. It tells them exactly which shelf, which product, and how long it's been empty.

5

They restock. Camera confirms.

Once your team restocks, the camera sees it and closes the alert automatically. Everything is logged so you can see response times in your reports.
