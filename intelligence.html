The goal is not faster restocking. The goal is shelves that never go empty. SmartStock learns why gaps happen and stops them before they start.

How It Works

Two layers. One system.

Preventing gaps from opening is the goal. When one does open anyway, it's closed in minutes. SmartStock runs both layers simultaneously from day one.

Layer 1 — Predictive · Ultimate Package

Stop the gap from
opening in the first place

Every alert is a data point. By week 8, SmartStock has modeled demand patterns for every SKU: peak hours, seasonal shifts, weather-correlated spikes. Reorder recommendations arrive before shelves go empty. The goal isn't a faster response. It's no gap at all.

01 AI builds 8-week demand forecast per SKU
02 Reorder recommendation pushed before depletion
03 Shelf is restocked before a gap opens
04 Forecast improves +15% accuracy by week 8
Alert volume drops over time. The system gets smarter. Gaps get rarer.
Layer 2 — Reactive · Both Packages

Close the gap
before the customer sees it

Without SmartStock, the average gap stays open 2–4 hours before anyone notices. The reactive layer cuts that to 4.2 minutes. Every gap that opens still gets closed — just faster than a customer can walk to the shelf.

01 Camera detects shelf gap in <30 sec
02 Push alert sent to responsible associate
03 Associate restocks — median 4.2 min
04 Camera confirms fill — alert auto-closed
OOS reduced to ~10% (Deluxe) · ~5% (Ultimate) · Payback in 6–7 months
