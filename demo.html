③ Mobile Alert

What the associate sees on their phone

The full loop, in 4 minutes or less

When the camera detects a gap, the right associate gets a push notification within 60 seconds — with the exact bay, SKU, and how long the gap has been open. They tap "Acknowledge," walk to the bay, restock, and the camera auto-confirms fill.

The customer sees a full shelf. The gap is logged, timestamped, and added to the weekly report. No one had to do a manual walkthrough to find it.

📷 Gap detected in <30 seconds
📱 Alert on phone in <60 seconds
📦 Median restock time: 4.2 minutes
✅ Camera auto-closes alert on confirm

Try it: tap Acknowledge to see the associate open the app, then tap Mark Restocked to close the loop and watch the alert resolve.
